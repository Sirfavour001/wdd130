Overview

Purpose

The primary purpose of a site rafting project is to reconfigure or optimize a physical location or site with the aim of achieving objectives such as improved efficiency, safety enhancement, infrastructure upgrades, environmental considerations, cost reduction, compliance with regulations, aesthetic improvement, and future-proofing.

Audience

Who need this project? The audience for a site rafting project includes project owners, managers, contractors, government agencies, environmental groups, the local community, investors, end users, safety inspectors, utility providers, environmental consultants, media, financial analysts, legal advisors, and the public. Effective communication with these stakeholders is vital for project transparency and success.
